an infection of the inner lining of the heart (endocardium), usually involving the heart valves. IE is caused by bacteria, fungi, or other microorganisms that enter the bloodstream and attach to damaged heart valves or other areas of the heart. Symptoms include fever, chills, fatigue, aching joints and muscles, night sweats, and shortness of breath. Treatment typically involves prolonged antibiotic therapy and, in some cases, surgery to repair or replace damaged heart valves.
